Professional Use Only

VentBox is designed exclusively for professional workplace interactions.

Our service screens concerns for improving workplace communications and professional relationships. This service is not currently designed to assist with personal matters, and emails regarding personal issues will not be responded to.

Target use case: Professional emails to coworkers, managers, colleagues, clients, or other business contacts.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Overview

VentBox is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

We will never sell your data. Not only because it's evil, but also because we don't even know how to.

Information We Collect

When you send an email to VentBox, we collect:

  • Email content (the message you send to clicksend@ventbox.net)
  • Your email address (to send you a response)
  • Email metadata (date, time, subject line if provided)

PII Sanitization

We have a filtering and screening layer that pseudonymizes any personally identifiable information (PII) that is detected. This includes names, phone numbers, addresses, employee IDs, and other identifying information. Your privacy is protected through automated PII detection and replacement.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Analyze the content of your email for safety scoring
  • Provide feedback and professional guidance
  • Send you a response with your results
  • Improve our analysis models (in anonymized, aggregated form)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All emails are processed through secure, encrypted channels. Your email content is confidential and will never be shared with third parties, employers, or any external entities.

Data Retention

Email content is retained only as long as necessary to provide our service and improve our models. Data is stored with pseudonyms in place. Data is routinely moved to an air-gapped storage system.
